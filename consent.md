### Purpose of the Study
We are students conducting research as part of a Design Thinking project. The goal of this study 
is to better understand how class attendance is currently tracked and managed, and to identify 
challenges faced by students, lecturers, and administrative staff.

---

### What Participation Involves
If you agree to participate, you will be asked to take part in a brief interview or observation 
session lasting approximately **20–30 minutes**. You may be asked questions about your experiences 
with attendance tracking, your routines, and any frustrations or suggestions you may have.

---

### Voluntary Participation
Your participation in this study is **entirely voluntary**. You may:
- Decline to answer any question at any time
- Withdraw from the study at any point without consequence
- Ask for your data to be excluded after the session

---

### Anonymity and Confidentiality
- Your **name will not be used** in any reports or presentations unless you explicitly give permission.
- Participants will be referred to using general roles (e.g., "Lecturer A", "Student B").
- Any notes, transcripts, or recordings will be stored securely and accessed only by the project team.
- Data will be used **solely for academic purposes** within this project and will not be shared externally.

---

### Data Usage
Information gathered will be used to:
- Build empathy maps and user personas
- Identify key insights and pain points
- Inform the definition of a design challenge

Data will **not** be used for any commercial purpose, published without consent, or shared with 
institutional authorities in a way that could identify you.
